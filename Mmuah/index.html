Café Mmuah

A Place for Every Moment

Located in Tallinn, Café Mmuah brings together Japanese inspired cuisine and European café traditions in a warm and welcoming atmosphere.

Our menu combines fresh ingredients, carefully prepared dishes and flavours created for every moment of the day. Enjoy a relaxed morning coffee, a long lunch, an evening dinner or a glass of wine with friends.

"Whether you visit for a quick coffee, a shared meal or a special evening, Mmuah is a place to slow down, connect and enjoy the moment."
